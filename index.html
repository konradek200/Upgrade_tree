How to play?

Gain fragment points, that you gain automatically.

Press any layers to show their upgrades and convert button.

Press convert button, to convert fragment points to points.

You can use points to buy upgrades, wich makes fragment points production faster.

If you gain enough points, you can do Rebirth, wich resets layers above, but it will give you rebirth points.

From rebirth points you can buy even more upgrades and strive for Prestige.

And in prestige the same thing as in other layers and it oges as long as other layers will be added.

Have fun!
